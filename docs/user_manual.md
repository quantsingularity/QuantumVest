## Platform Overview

QuantumVest combines artificial intelligence, blockchain technology, and quantitative finance models to provide predictive analytics for investment decision-making. The platform analyzes historical market data, blockchain transactions, and various economic indicators to generate predictions and investment recommendations.

### Key Features

- **AI-Powered Predictions**: Get accurate forecasts for stock and cryptocurrency price movements
- **Portfolio Optimization**: Receive recommendations for optimal asset allocation based on your risk profile
- **Blockchain Transparency**: Track transactions and analyze market trends using blockchain data
- **Quantitative Analysis**: Access sophisticated financial models typically available only to institutional investors
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Visualize complex data through intuitive charts and graphs

## Getting Started

### Account Creation

1. Navigate to the QuantumVest homepage
2. Click on "Sign Up" in the top-right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email address through the confirmation link
5. Complete your profile by providing additional information

### Login

1. Click on "Login" in the top-right corner
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Login" to access your dashboard

### Account Recovery

If you forget your password:

1. Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password

## Dashboard

The Dashboard is your central hub for monitoring your investments and accessing platform features.

### Dashboard Components

- **Market Overview**: Current market trends and key indicators
- **Your Portfolio**: Summary of your investment portfolio
- **Recent Predictions**: Latest AI-generated predictions
- **Performance Metrics**: Key performance indicators for your investments
- **News Feed**: Relevant financial news that may impact your investments

### Customizing Your Dashboard

1. Click on the "Customize" button in the top-right corner of the Dashboard
2. Drag and drop widgets to rearrange them
3. Click on the gear icon on any widget to configure its settings
4. Click "Save" to apply your changes

## Prediction Charts

The Prediction Charts feature provides AI-generated forecasts for various assets.

### Accessing Prediction Charts

1. Click on "Prediction Charts" in the main navigation menu
2. Select the asset type (Stocks or Cryptocurrencies)
3. Choose a specific asset from the dropdown menu
4. Select the time frame for the prediction (1 day, 1 week, 1 month, 3 months)

### Understanding the Charts

- **Historical Data**: Represented by the solid line
- **Prediction**: Represented by the dashed line
- **Confidence Interval**: Shaded area around the prediction line
- **Key Events**: Marked with icons on the chart

### Exporting Predictions

1. Click on the "Export" button above the chart
2. Select your preferred format (PDF, CSV, or PNG)
3. Choose whether to include analysis notes
4. Click "Download"

## Portfolio Optimization

The Portfolio Optimization feature helps you allocate your investments optimally based on your risk tolerance and financial goals.

### Creating an Optimization Plan

1. Click on "Portfolio Optimization" in the main navigation menu
2. Enter your current holdings or start from scratch
3. Specify your risk tolerance (Conservative, Moderate, Aggressive)
4. Set your investment goals and time horizon
5. Click "Generate Optimization Plan"

### Implementing Recommendations

1. Review the recommended asset allocation
2. Click on "Implementation Guide" for step-by-step instructions
3. Choose whether to implement all recommendations or select specific ones
4. Track the performance of your optimized portfolio over time

## Settings

The Settings section allows you to configure your account preferences and platform settings.

### Account Settings

## Notifications

QuantumVest provides timely notifications about important events and predictions.

### Types of Notifications

- **Prediction Alerts**: Notifications when new predictions are available
- **Market Alerts**: Alerts about significant market movements
- **Portfolio Updates**: Updates about your portfolio performance
- **System Announcements**: Information about platform updates and maintenance

### Managing Notifications

1. Click on the bell icon in the top navigation bar to view recent notifications
2. Click on "View All" to see your notification history
3. Go to Settings > Notification Preferences to configure your notification settings

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How accurate are the predictions?**
A: Our AI models have demonstrated an average accuracy of 70-75% in predicting directional movements. However, all predictions come with confidence intervals to indicate the level of certainty.

**Q: Is my financial data secure?**
A: Yes, we employ industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. We do not share your personal financial information with third parties.

**Q: Can I use QuantumVest on mobile devices?**
A: Yes, QuantumVest is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. We also offer dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What data sources does QuantumVest use?**
A: We aggregate data from multiple sources including market data providers, blockchain networks, financial news APIs, and economic indicators from governmental sources.

**Q: How often are predictions updated?**
A: Short-term predictions (1-day forecasts) are updated hourly, while longer-term predictions are updated daily.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, you can export your portfolio data, predictions, and analytics in various formats including PDF, CSV, and Excel.
